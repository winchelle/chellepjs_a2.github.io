Photography

As I saw a lot of places, I started to like taking pictures of the places I've been to. I love when I can look back and see those pictures I took. Sometimes, I would rather have a lot of pictures of the scenery than take a picture of myself. Taking photos helps me reminisce about the places, that's why I love photography.
